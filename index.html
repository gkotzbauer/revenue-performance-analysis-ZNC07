Revenue Cycle Performance Past Ten (10) Weeks

Charge Balance Btwn Zero Balance (ZB) & Collection Rate

The total charge balance per week using the formula ((Zero Balance - Collection) * Charges). A negative balance means the collection rate is higher than the zero balance (ZB) rate. The goal is to be zero or positive.

Zero Balance (ZB) Collection Rate

The Zero Balance (ZB) collection rate is the percentage of the billed charges collected for all closed (zeroed) accounts that are not classified as rebill.

Charges Denial %

The Charges Denial % is the average percentage of denials for all charges in a week.

% of Remaining Billed Charges

The % of Remaining Billed Charges is the Charge Billed Balance divided by the Charge Amount. A lower percentage is better.

Zero-Balance Collection Narrative

The classifications by payer represent the number of times the Zero-Balance Collection for the payer falls into each category over the last 10 weeks.

Net Revenue Per Visit Gap

The Net Revenue Per Visit Gap is weekly sum of the NRV Zero Balance for the week minus Payments per Visit for the week. A positive balance indicates positive performance.
